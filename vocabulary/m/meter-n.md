# meter (n)

/ˈmiːtə(r)/

## &bull; a [device](../d/device-n.md#•-an-object-or-a-piece-of-equipment-that-has-been-designed-to-do-a-particular-job) that [measures](../m/measure-v.md#•-to-find-the-size-quantity-etc-of-something-in-standard-units) and [records](../r/record-v.md#•-to-keep-a-permanent-account-of-facts-or-events-by-writing-them-down-filming-them-storing-them-in-a-computer-etc) the amount of [electricity](../e/electricity-n.md#•-a-form-of-energy-from-charged-elementary-particles-usually-supplied-as-electric-current-through-cables-wires-etc-for-lighting-heating-driving-machines-ect), gas, water, etc. that you have used or the time and distance you have travelled, etc.
