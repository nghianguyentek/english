# signal (n)

/ˈsɪɡnəl/

## &bull; a movement or sound that you [make](../m/make-v.md#•-to-write-create-or-prepare-something) to give [somebody](somebody-p.md#•-a-person-who-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name) [information](../i/information-n.md#•-facts-or-details-about-somebody-or-something), [instructions](../i/instruction-n.md#•-a-piece-of-information-that-tells-a-computer-to-perform-a-particular-operation), a warning, etc. (dấu hiệu)

*Synonym: sign*

## &bull; a series of [electrical](../e/electrical-adj.md#•-connected-with-electricity-using-or-producing-electricity) waves that carry sounds, pictures, or [messsages](../m/message-n.md#•-a-piece-of-information-sent-in-electrical-form-for-example-by-email-or-mobile), for example to a radio, television, or [mobile phone](../m/mobile-n.md#•-a-mobile-phone) (tín hiệu)

## &bull; an [event](../e/event-n.md#•-a-thing-that-happens-especially-something-important), an action, a [fact](../f/fact-n.md#•-a-thing-that-is-known-to-be-true-especially-when-it-can-be-proved), etc. that [shows](../s/show-v.md#•-to-give-particular-information-or-a-time-or-measurement) that [something](something-p.md#•-a-thing-that-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name) [exists](../e/exist-v.md#•-to-be-real-to-be-present-in-a-place-or-situation) or is likely to [happen](../h/happen-v.md#•-to-take-place-especially-without-being-planned)

*Synonym: indication*
