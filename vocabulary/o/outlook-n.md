# outlook (n)

/ˈaʊtlʊk/ [🔊](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/media/english/uk_pron/o/out/outlo/outlook__gb_1.mp3) [🔊](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/media/english/us_pron/o/out/outlo/outlook__us_1.mp3)

## the attitude to life and the world of a particular person, group, or culture (cách nhìn; quan điểm)

- positive/optimistic/pessimistic/bleak/gloomy outlook
- outlook on something
- in outlook

## (Doubt, guessing, and certainty) the likely future for somebody/something; what is likely to happen (triển vọng)

Synonym [prospect](../p/prospect)
