# assemble (v)

/əˈsembl/

## &bull; to come together as a group; to bring [people](../p/people-n.md#•-humans-in-general-everyone) or [things](../t/thing-n.md#•-an-object-that-is-not-alive) together as a group (tập hợp)

## &bull; (assemble something) to fit together all the separate parts of something, for example a piece of furniture (ráp)

*Opposite: disassemble*
