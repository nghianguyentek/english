# assemble (v)

/əˈsembl/

## &bull; to come together as a [group](../g/group-n.md#•-a-number-of-people-or-things-that-are-together-in-the-same-place-or-that-are-connected-in-some-way); to bring [people](../p/people-n.md#•-humans-in-general-everyone) or [things](../t/thing-n.md#•-an-object-that-is-not-alive) together as a [group](../g/group-n.md#•-a-number-of-people-or-things-that-are-together-in-the-same-place-or-that-are-connected-in-some-way) (tập hợp)

## &bull; (assemble something) to fit together all the separate [parts](../p/part-n.md#•-part-of-something-some-but-not-all-of-a-thing) of [something](../s/something-p.md#•-a-thing-that-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name), for example a piece of furniture (ráp)

*Opposite: disassemble*
