# workflow (n)

/ˈwɜːkfləʊ/

## &bull; the series of stages that a [particular](../p/particular-adj.md#•-used-to-emphasize-that-you-are-referring-to-one-individual-person-thing-or-type-of-thing-and-not-others) piece or [type](../t/type-n.md#•-a-class-or-group-of-people-or-things-that-share-particular-qualities-or-features-and-are-part-of-a-larger-group-a-kind-or-sort) of [work](../w/work-n.md#•-the-duties-that-you-have-and-the-activities-that-you-do-as-a-part-of-your-job) passes through from the beginning until it is finished; the rate at which it passes through these stages
