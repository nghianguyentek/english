# resource (n)

## &bull; a supply of [something](../s/something-p.md#•-a-thing-that-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name) that a country, an organization, or a [person](../p/person-n.md#•-a-human-as-an-individual) has and can use, [especially](../e/especially-adv.md#•-for-a-particular-purpose-person-etc) to increase their wealth

## &bull; [something](../s/something-p.md#•-a-thing-that-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name) that can be used to help achieve an aim, [especially](../e/especially-adv.md#•-for-a-particular-purpose-person-etc) a book, [equipment](../e/equipment-n.md#•-the-things-that-are-needed-for-a-particular-purpose-or-activity), etc. that provides [information](../i/information-n.md#•-facts-or-details-about-somebody-or-something) for techers and students
