# resource (n)

/rɪˈsɔːs/ or /rɪˈzɔːs/

## &bull; a [supply](../s/supply-n.md#•-an-amount-of-something-that-is-provided-or-available-to-be-used) of [something](../s/something-p.md#•-a-thing-that-is-not-known-or-mentioned-by-name) that a country, an organization, or a [person](../p/person-n.md#•-a-human-as-an-individual) has and can use, [especially](../e/especially-adv.md#•-for-a-particular-purpose-person-etc) to [increase](../i/increase-v.md#•-to-become-greater-in-amount-number-value-etc-to-make-something-greater-in-amount-number-value-etc) their wealth
