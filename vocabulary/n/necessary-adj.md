# necessary (adj)

/ˈnesəsəri/

## &bull; that is needed for a [purpose](../p/purpose-n.md#•-the-intention-aim-or-function-of-something-the-things-that-something-is-supposed-to-achieve) or a reason

*Synonym: essential*

*Opposite: [unnecessary](../u/unnecessary-adj.md#•-not-needed-not-more-than-is-needed)*

## &bull; that must [exist](../e/exist-v.md#•-to-be-real-to-be-present-in-a-place-or-situation) or [happen](../h/happen-v.md#•-to-take-place-especially-without-being-planned) and cannot be avoided

*Synonym: inevitable*
